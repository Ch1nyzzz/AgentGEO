    Herbs to Fight Against Breast Cancer Naturally

    Today we will tell you about plants and herbs to fight against breast cancer. Breast cancer is a commonly found in women. Many of you would be surprised as well as shocked to know that every year more than 100,000 cases of breast cancer are reported. The basic treatments for breast cancer include surgery, radiation or chemotherapy, it is a delightful fact to know that there are certain herbs which can have positive effects and results for palliating breast cancer. Take a quick glance and find out these amazing herbs which fight against breast cancer.

    Herbs to Fight Against Breast Cancer

    #1 Astragalus (Huang qi) – This wonderful drug has its origin in China. It is known and valued for enhancing the production of interferon in the body and that too in a natural way. The research and studies conducted on this herb showed wonderful results wherein the survival possibilities just doubled when this herb was used with the other breast cancer treatment. This herb aids the body’s immune system to recognize the cancer cells and help control them.

     

    #2 Berberis – The herbs containing berberine are known to enhance the effectiveness of breast cancer and other cancer treatment drugs. For example, Podophyllum peltatum contains berberine which helps fight against the breast cancer cells. In the earlier research, it has been observed that berberine did even better than cancer drugs when a study was conducted for brain cancer cases. The berberine ingredient improves the effectiveness of breast cancer drugs also.

    #3 Sanguinaria Canadensis – The common name for this herb is bloodroot. The best advantage that has been proven through studies for bloodroot is that it is very helpful to combat cancer cells. The different research studies have shown that bloodroot herb reduces the size of cancer tumors and increases possibilities of survival through cancer.  This is one of the most important herbs to fight against breast cancer. A significant fact about this herb is that it is used in black salve, an anti-cancer poultice used for breast cancer.

    Also Read About These Cancer Fighting Foods

    #4 Ruscus Aculeatus – Commonly, known as Butcher’s broom, this herb contains active ingredients that are used to combat cancer. Research on this herb has shown that the active ingredients contained in this herb have valuable abilities to shrink the cancer cells. Because of the efficient abilities, this herb is used for the treatment of breast cancer.

    #5 Uncaria Tormentosa – The common name for this herb is cat’s claw.  This herb stimulates and improves the immunity system and works as an adaptogen, thereby, helping in cleaning the white blood cells (WBCs). One of the significant herbs to fight against breast cancer, the Uncaria tormentosa herb helps decrease the size of cancer tumors. Another significant fact about this amazing herb is that it also helps fade away the side effects of other cancer treatments like chemotherapy and radiotherapy.

    #6 Allium Sativum – The common name for this is garlic! Since many years, garlic has been used effectively for treating various ailments. The garlic oil contains ajoene which reduces the cancer production. Also, it contains selenium, cyaniding, quercetin, and bioflavonoids that have wonderful antioxidant properties. The rich amounts of organic sulfides, as well as polysulfide, fills garlic with significant anticancer activities that help fight various cancers including the breast cancer. The anti-cancer activities help kill the cancer cells by reducing the metabolic activity of these cells.

    #7 Curcuma longa – The common name of this herb will instantly make you recognize it! It is turmeric! This dark yellow colored herb is full of curcumin which is an active ingredient for fighting various cancers including skin cancer, breast cancer, and stomach cancer. The curcumin, in turn, is full of phenolic substances that help control the cancer cells.

    #8 Burdock – The scientific term for this herb is Arctium lappa. Mainly found in Asia and Europe, this herb has been in therapeutic uses since old times. It is useful for treating ailments like measles, tonsillitis, and arthritis. It is also useful to fight and reduce cancer. The active ingredients of burdock are efficient to bring changes in oncogenes. Due to this unique property, burdock is used to combat ovary cancer, breast cancer, and bladder cancer also. The use of this herb has shown a reduction in cancer tumor size and pain. It has also shown better chances of survival. The Arctigenin substance of burdock has the capability of removing low nutrient tumor cells. Burdock also contains Tannin which is one of the most important phenolic compounds to fight breast cancer.

    #9 Camellia sinensis – We all know this herb by the name of the green tea. The polyphenolics compounds of green tea are responsible for the anti-cancer activity found in it. EGGG or Epigallocatechin is a polyphenol that helps in anti-tumor and antimutagenic activity. The EGGG is present in a small amount in green tea.

    #10 Cimicifuga Recemosa – The common name for Cimicifuga recemosa is black cohosh. Mainly, this shrub is found in North America forests. Those having breast cancer often use black cohosh with chemo and radiotherapy. It is also worth mentioning that this shrub has been in treatment uses since old times. Today, a comprehensive range of preparation of black cohosh can be found in varied drug shops.

    Apart from these important herbs to fight against breast cancer, it is strongly recommended that the patients should have a proper nutritious diet as per the advice of their doctor. The green leafy vegetables and fresh fruits have ingredients that help prevent breast cancer. Also, patients should learn and understand immunotherapy as it is a great option that naturally enhances the body’s immune system. It also has negligible side effects in comparison to other cancer treatments.
