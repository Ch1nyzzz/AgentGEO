Description

Liver Cancer is a disease of the Liver.
The abnormal growth of blood vessels in the Liver give rise to Cancer.

Functions of Liver are
It produces cholesterol
Production of hormones
It produces bile salts that help in digestion
It filters the toxic substances from the blood
It stores minerals and vitamin A, D, K, B12
It helps to maintain the proper level of glucose in the blood
Processing and storage of iron for production of red blood cells
Manufacture of cholesterol and other chemicals required for fat transport
It converts the waste product of the body into urea and excrete them out in urine

Cancer affects the functions of the Liver.
Cancer that originates in the Liver is Primary Liver Cancer.
Cancer that originates in other body parts but spreads to the Liver is Secondary Liver Cancer.

Obesity
Alcohol

Diabetes

Hepatitis, Hepatitis A,B,C,D,E
Cancers of other parts such as Bowel, Pancreas, Stomach, Lung, Kidney, Ovarian, Prostrate or Breast

If not treated properly, Liver Cancer may cause
Death
