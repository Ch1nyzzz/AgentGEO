Human Capital Advisory

Empowering workforces. Shaping business outcomes.

Amid changing workplace models and adoption of emerging technologies, organizations must manage increasingly complex workforce issues to adapt and thrive. KPMG Human Capital Advisory services can provide your organization with innovative, cohesive people solutions and insights and drive accelerated, sustainable business performance. Harness our expertise with enterprise organization transformation, innovative talent strategies, HR, Payroll, Learning function optimization and implementation of all enabling technologies to develop a future-ready workforce that fuels growth and success for your organization.

Our approach to workforce strategy centers on the transformative impact of generative AI, labor market and talent pool analysis, learning process transformation, and targeted professional development and upskilling. From HR strategy optimization to digital automation to operating model modernization, we offer HR, payroll and workforce management solutions that align with organizations’ the digital transformation agendas.

Embrace the future with KPMG Human Capital Advisory and experience a more innovative and sustainable approach to building an agile, future-ready organization.

Explore our services

Build a world-class HR organization to support your business and develop your next generation workforce enabled by generative AI and other emerging technologies.

Digital Learning

This offering drives business value by enabling and leveraging technology, such as GenAI, to provide engaging, connected and efficient learning experiences. Upskilling and reskilling knowledge workers is critical to your organization’s growth and success in a rapidly changing market. Together, we design a learning strategy aligned with your business strategy and provide tech-enabled learning experiences.

Digital Workforce Experience Transformation

Our teams help organizations redefine the workforce experience with technology solutions that increase productivity, enhance business results, and meet the needs of the current talent agenda. Address the full lifecycle of technology-enabled programs, from vision and strategy, to implementation and optimization.

GenAI Workforce Transformation

A cross-functional team helps clients redefine digital transformation programs in a human-centered way unlocking the power of GenAI to deliver on the workforce value proposition, transform how work gets done across the enterprise, evolve how teams are organized, and improve culture and employee experience.

Read more

Global HR Operating Model Transformation

We help organizations transition to a best-in class human capital (hire-to-retire) by defining how services are delivered (e.g. GenAI, automation, bots) deliver human capital services globally, regionally or locally to your employee population.

Global Payroll

One of our most robust offerings, guides clients through the transformation of the entire Payroll Value Stream using proprietary tools and methodologies. Optimize Operating and Service Delivery Models; vendor and technology selection; roadmap creation and implementation; and diagnostic, remediation and implementation services for payroll technologies.

Learning as a Service (LaaS)

Our Learning as a Service offering focus on learning strategy optimization and managed solutions that comprise target operating model transformation, managed services, and learning academies. Our award-winning global learning advisors provide the blueprints needed to execute organizations’ future vision, while advanced technology tools including automation, generative AI (GenAI), adaptive learning, and advanced analytics help deliver optimal business impact and employee experience.

Read more

Organization Transformation

This service offering leverages a human-centric and systematic approach to aligning an organization’s structure and ways of working to the business strategy, while clearly defining roles, decision-making processes, and capabilities to enable the new organizational design.

Strategic Workforce Planning

We provide a critical framework for organizations to navigate the integration of Artificial Intelligence technologies, particularly GenAI, into their businesses. Ensure technology and human capital are aligned by anticipating supply and demand for skills over time, centralizing skills, where appropriate; and establishing the right balance between human and AI workers using predictive analytics and scenario planning.

Talent Strategies & Transformation

This service encompasses a robust set of data-driven workforce solutions designed to help organizations tackle critical challenges, unlock value, and develop a future-ready workforce aligned with long-term business goals.

Workforce Management

We help businesses evaluate and implement systems, tools, and workflows to boost efficiency and productivity. Equally critical are reporting mechanisms to capture real-time updates on company performance, ensure adequate staffing based on demand, and monitor work performance.
