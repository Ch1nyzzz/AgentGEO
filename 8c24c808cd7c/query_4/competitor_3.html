Salesforce for Nonprofits FAQ

CRM is technology to manage a Nonprofit's relationships and interactions with stakeholders, from donors and program participants to volunteers and clients. Nonprofit CRM software improves relationship management by creating a 360-degree view of everyone, capturing their interactions with the organization.

CRM for Nonprofit eliminates the inefficiency of having data spread across multiple systems. With a Nonprofit CRM in place, organizations can collate necessary information under a single umbrella and deliver personalized experiences to all of their stakeholders on one, integrated platform.

Through the Power of Us Program any eligible nonprofit receives 10 free Salesforce Nonprofit Cloud or Sales/Service Cloud licenses. For additional licenses, Salesforce offers discounts to eligible Nonprofit. Nonprofit get the same functionality as other Salesforce customers at a lower cost.

There are several options available for nonprofits to get started with Salesforce solutions. The Power of Us Program gives eligible Nonprofit the first 10 subscriptions free. Visit our pricing page for more information.
