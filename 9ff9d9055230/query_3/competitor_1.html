Beijing Xian Tours 2026 - Travel Two Greatest Ancient Capitals Together

  • Visit the two greatest ancient China's capital cities - Beijing and Xian in just one go!

    If you have been intrigued by the history and mysteries of China's tremendous past for a long time, Beijing and Xian are the two places you must go first. Beijing is a modern metropolitan on the surface, but beneath the surface is innumerable amazing history and culture hidden in the palaces, temples, imperial gardens, museums, Hutong as well as the magnificent Great Wall. Xian, served as the capital for more than 1,000 years through 13 ancient dynasties and the eastern starting point of the ancient Silk Road, is the birthplace of Chinese civilization and home to Terracotta Warriors and Horses and many other historical wonders. Besides the rich tourism resources, both Beijing and Xian provide great hotels, stylish food, and convenient transportation. You can visit them during any season of the year.

    How to Plan a Beijing Xian tour? You can begin your tour either from Beijing or from Xian. Beijing has more international flights, domestic flights and trains than Xian. Xian is usually the second or next stop of a China trip. The distance between Beijing and Xian is about 1200 KM(746 miles), approximately 2hrs by flight, 4~5hrs by high speed bullet train and 12~15 hours by overnight normal trains. Generally speaking, you are suggested to stay 3~4 days in Beijing and 2~3 days in Xian.
