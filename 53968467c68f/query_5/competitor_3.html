    Breathe Polarizingly,

    An Essential Component Of Your Indoor Air Quality. Air filters are in every heating and cooling unit, preventing significant pollutants from passing through the system and into your breathing air. Unfortunately, the average filters that homeowners might pick up at their local grocery store leave much to be desired when it comes to absolute filtration.

    Why Use EnviroAire IAQ Media Air Cleaners?

    Unlike traditional air filters, EnviroAire IAQ Cleaners are designed to perform at a much finer filtration rate, greatly maximizing your clean-air potential while capturing sub-micron contaminants that other filters leave behind. EnviroAire IAQ Cleaners boast high-efficiency ratings to help ensure the smooth operation of your HVAC system, saving you money on future repairs.

    The 1″ Polarized-Media Electronic Air Cleaner utilizes a combination of low static pressure and a polarized media pad to attract and capture potentially toxic sub-micron contaminants and VOCs (Volatile Organic Compounds) from entering your home’s airflow through the HVAC system.

    EnviroAire IAQ’s Polarized-Media Air Cleaner is easily installed in your existing HVAC filter tracks. Unlike many other air filtration products, EnviroAire Cleaners don’t require any ductwork modifications, making installation simple, cost-effective, and convenient.

    How Big Is A Micron?

    While the average filter only traps larger forms of contaminants, EnviroAire IAQ Cleaners capture tiny microns in the air, making them far more effective. Microns are small, allowing them to permeate their environment more detrimentally, but with EnviroAire IAQ Cleaners, you don’t need to worry about millions of sub-microns infecting your home!

    HOW DOES THE 1″ ENVIROAIRE IAQ FILTER WORK?

    As the air from your home pulls into your HVAC system, sub-microns become polarized. Once polarized, they have a magnetic pull toward other harmful particles, attracting spores to themselves to create larger contaminants. These large molecules are then trapped on a disposable, low-static polarized media pad within the filter. The pad uses an activated charcoal center screen for optimum cleaning power, making EnviroAire Cleaners the ultimate germ-filtering system for your home.

    The EnviroAire IAQ 1″ Polarized Media Air Cleaner comes in multiple sizes to fit your needs (ModelsCLP1620, CLP1625, CLP2020, and CLP2025). To find the right option for you, have an EnviroAire IAQ Certified Partner out to your home to determine the best fit.

    MERV Rating Breakdown

    MERV is an acronym for Minimum Efficiency Reporting Value.

    The MERV rates the overall efficiency and effectiveness of the filter you use in your home.

    Air filters have holes that allow air and particles to pass through them. The smaller the holes, the more contaminants that are filtered. Smaller holes = higher MERV value. EnviroAire IAQ Cleaner’s high MERV ratings mean they have a finer filtration system, allowing cleaner air to push into your home.

    1“ Polarized filter when combined with the polarized unit has the equivalent MERV rating as MERV 13.
