Who we are

The DGHA is a network of organizations based in the Netherlands working on global health. With members rooted in various local contexts, we draw on our diverse expertise and knowledge to coordinate advocacy efforts and inform Dutch policymaking. Together, we bring interdisciplinary and practical perspectives to encourage coherent and data-driven policies that promote health for all.

What we do

Joint advocacy

We coordinate actions, and amplify the advocacy efforts of knowledge and civil society organizations.

Data-driven advocacy

We provide thought-leadership, translating expertise from the ground into policy recommendations. We strive to keep global health as a priority.

Global health for all

We stand for equity and advocate for policies that leave no one behind. Because health is a human right.

Knowledge platform

We provide a platform to exchange and build knowledge among members and grow networks of global health advocates.
