Global MOOC and Online Education Alliance key activities

Global MOOC and Online Education Alliance serves as a mechanism for exchange and cooperation, providing leadership of global higher education efforts to address digital education challenges and to implement practical policies in local communities and around the world. Key activities include:

Joint Teaching

We encourage and strengthen bilateral or multilateral cooperation for fully online/ blended/ hybrid courses and lifelong education programs development.

Capacity Building

We encourage and strengthen the cooperation to progress research, develop tools, cultivate talents, and teacher training in the field of MOOC and online education.

Knowledge Sharing

We regularly release online newsletters, as well as host webinars (expert panels, workshops, showcases), academic conferences, and meetings to share experiences and best practices.

Public Advocacy (SDG 4)

We guide the transformation of public perception and build consensus about the development of MOOC and online education in higher education, including public awareness regarding UN SDG 4 (Quality Education).
