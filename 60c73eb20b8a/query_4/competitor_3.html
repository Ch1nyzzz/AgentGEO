Decorating Den Interiors - Franchise

A Beautiful Business with Interior Design. As the world's largest franchisor of Interior Design, we offer a turnkey business that includes comprehensive training, over 130 Wholesale Suppliers of Furniture, Window Treatments, Floorcovering, Wallcovering, Accessories & much more. All products come in Good/Better/Best quality and price so we can meet any realistic client budget. Proven Marketing Systems and lots of on-going support. We have proprietary business software to run a solid business.

What Does a Decorating Den Interiors Franchise Cost?

Interested parties should have at least $50,000 in liquid capital to invest and a minimum net worth of $99,000. Franchisees can expect to make a total investment of at least $59,900. Decorating Den Interiors charges a franchise fee of $39,900. They also offer financing as well as a discount for veterans ($499 training fee is waived for any veteran). *

The Beautiful Business of Interior Design

Be Your Own DecorBoss

For decades, we have helped talented individuals turn their passion into a profitable business. There’s no need to reinvent the wheel! Our concept is simple: we provide our Design Franchise Partners with all the training, tools, suppliers, systems and support necessary to run a rewarding interior decorating/design business and to outshine the competition.

Training & Support

Here are just a few of the ways we help existing interior decorators & designers, those shifting careers, or others considering college, design school, or going straight into the workforce as a DecorBoss.

Professional Training
The training provided to our Designer Franchise Partners is comprehensive and ongoing. Whether someone joins us with just an “eye for design”, or already has a design degree and experience, our programs and trainers are here to support the needs of every new DecorBoss (aka. business owner).

Business Management
Learn how to effectively manage a design business to be profitable and successful without the struggle of starting from scratch or maintaining everything on your own.

Mentorship + Coaching
Decorating Den Interiors coaches and mentors are available to ensure your business gets off to the right start and keeps on growing.

Wholesale Buying Network
With over 135 brand name manufacturers and suppliers, our vendor relationships and enormous buying power allow designers and decorators to obtain true wholesale pricing instead of “to the trade” pricing.

Marketing
Even the best decorators and designers need the phone to ring to be successful. Our franchise comes with turn-key systems for marketing and generating new clients.

National Advertisements
We feature our DecorBoss family in monthly full-page ads in national magazines such as ELLE DÉCOR, Traditional Home, House Beautiful or Veranda. Next time, it could be your work before millions of eyeballs throughout America.

Television + Sirius Radio
If you're seriously into television advertising, we can help there too. We provide our DecorBoss owners with commercials ready to air. Nationally, we advertise on Sirius FM.

Social Media Marketing
Don’t have time for tweeters or the #instaworld? Don’t worry! We'll not only create the content but we'll update it weekly for you too! All you have to do is answer the calls or direct messages.

Public Relations
We gather the latest images from project installations from our entire DecorBoss family and present it to leading national and local editors in your area. DDI has a professionally connected PR guru working for your good name at all times.

Graphic Designer
Need an ad for a local campaign or custom social media graphic for an event or project? No problem. Our graphic design team is at your service and we’ll help maintain a beautiful brand presence.

Our Ideal Candidate

We are looking for franchise candidates who have the innate talent (the "eye") for design, as well as strong people skills. This turnkey business does require some discipline and drive to achieve high levels of success. We have excellent training programs included so we routinely have people come to us from other careers and educational levels. Traits we look for include: addicted to HGTV, reads Design Magazines, frequent visitor to HOUZZ and/or Pinterest. Friends and family ask for design advice.
