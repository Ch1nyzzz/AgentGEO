Seattle’s Interior Design School

Launch your interior design career in 3 to 6 months

Our interior design school, located in the heart of Seattle, provides aspiring designers the opportunity to cultivate their natural design talents through hands-on learning programs in a supportive classroom environment. Our curriculum enables students to transform their passion into an extraordinary design portfolio. Students build confidence as they acquire the knowledge and skills required to succeed professionally in the industry.

Fundamentals of Interior Design

Turn your passion for design into a professional career with our Fundamentals of Interior Design Program. This dynamic program is your pathway to the interior design industry, combining hands-on projects with essential knowledge in color theory, design history, lighting, the design process, socially responsible design and so much more. Now featuring newly integrated AI tools to support your creative process and prepare you for the future of the industry. 

You’ll embark on an exciting journey of four residential design projects, each building your portfolio and refining your unique style. These projects lead to the pinnacle of the program—designing a full residential home. From concept to presentation, you’ll apply everything you’ve learned to create a stunning, cohesive, and functional residential space.

The program immerses you in the local design community through weekly field visits to showrooms, design firms, manufacturers and more, where you’ll connect with industry experts and gain valuable networking opportunities. 

Tuition: $8,995

Professional Fast-Track Program

This accelerated program combines our comprehensive Fundamentals of Interior Design course with one specialized technical course of your choice, empowering you to kickstart your design career in just three months. Choose from industry-leading options including AutoCAD, SketchUp, Photoshop, Illustrator and InDesign, Chief Architect, or Revit to build the technical skills needed for success.

Begin your journey today toward a fulfilling and impactful career in interior design. With a solid blend of creative and technical expertise, this program equips you to turn your vision into reality and build a career you love.

Tuition: $9,499*

*The tuition for the Fast-Track Program includes one elective class at a discounted rate. This provides you with the opportunity to further specialize and enhance your skills while keeping your education within reach.

Master Certification Program

Students receive a Professional Certificate in Interior Design once they complete the Master of Interior Design Certification Program. The Master of Interior Design Certification Program combines the Fundamentals of Interior Design Program with 9 electives as well as a final portfolio presentation to give students a well-rounded education and competitive portfolio.

The Master’s Certification Program requires completion of the following courses:

    • Fundamentals of Interior Design
    • AutoCAD
    • SketchUp
    • Photoshop, Illustrator & InDesign
    • Advanced 3D Modeling (Choose 1): Revit or Chief Architect – New!
    • Commercial Design
    • Construction Basics – New!
    • Business & Entrepreneurship
    • Kitchen & Bath Design
    • Master’s Portfolio Capstone

Tuition: $17,999

»Learn More

Upon completion of the Master Certification Program, graduates are eligible to complete the RIDQC exam to become certified. Learn more about RIDQC here.

Advanced Master Certification Program

Students who complete the Advanced Master of Interior Design Certification Program gain strong skills in both 3D rendering and hand rendering. This comprehensive program provides students with a Professional Certificate in Interior Design upon completion.

The Advanced Master of Interior Design Certification Program combines the foundational Fundamentals of Interior Design Program with an additional 11 elective courses. This well-rounded curriculum, along with a final portfolio presentation, ensures students develop a competitive and impressive portfolio.

The Advanced Master’s Certification Program requires completion of the following courses:

    • Fundamentals of Interior Design
    • AutoCAD
    • SketchUp
    • Hand Rendering
    • Photoshop, Illustrator & InDesign
    • Chief Architect – New!
    • Kitchen & Bath Design
    • Revit
    • Commercial Design
    • Construction Basics – New!
    • Business & Entrepreneurship
    • Master’s Portfolio Capstone

Tuition: $19,599

»Learn More

Upon completion of the Advanced Master Certification Program, graduates are eligible to complete the RIDQC exam to become certified. Learn more about RIDQC here.

Individual courses may also be taken by students not enrolled in the Master Certification Program, and by industry professionals with relevant design education or work experience. Read more about all of our courses on the Course Descriptions tab.
