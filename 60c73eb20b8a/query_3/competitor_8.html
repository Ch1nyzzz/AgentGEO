Frequently Asked Questions

Can I access the course materials after completing the course?

Yes, once you enroll, you have lifetime access to the course materials, allowing you to revisit and review at your own pace.

Are there any prerequisites for the course?

No, our Interior Design course is suitable for beginners and enthusiasts alike. We start from the basics and gradually progress to advanced topics.

How does the course work? What is the format?

This course is fully online, and there are several lessons. Each module consists of a video presentation with a recording and will be approximately 15 minutes long. After every module, there is a quiz that will help you fully understand the material.

Can I do the course in a language other than English?

At this time, the course is only offered in English.

Is the course free?

The course is free.
