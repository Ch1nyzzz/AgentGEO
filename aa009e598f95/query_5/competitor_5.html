About YJJ Travel Corp.

YJJ Travel Corp. Is An Affordable Luxury Travel Agency.

YJJ Travel Corp. was founded in New York City by Yajaida and Joshua Jordan in 2017.  The recently married couple were inspired by their frequent international travels to over 45 destinations collectively. They decided to open a travel agency and share their life changing experiences with the world.

Pack your bags and get ready for your next adventure. International destinations are re-opening their borders.  We’re just as excited as you to explore our big, beautiful world again. Allow YJJ Travel Corp. to assist with customizing travel packages based on the open destinations for US travelers.  Our objective is to provide you with a safe and affordable luxurious travel experience.  As a company that has extensive experience with groups, tell your co-workers, friends or family members and explore all the offers available.
