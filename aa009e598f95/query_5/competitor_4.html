    Adventure Travel Holidays

    What kind of adventure travel gets your blood pumping? Is it a safari in Botswana or watching the annual wildebeest migration in Kenya? Maybe hiking the Inca Trail is more your style. And if you’re looking for an adrenaline rush, you will find plenty of hair-raising activities in our collection of adventure packages. From white-water rafting on the Zambezi River to enjoying a Big 5 walking safari in the Kruger Park, we have the perfect adventure holiday package for you.   


    Adventure travel is out there with Thompsons Holidays  

    • Go diving in the Maldives, Bali, Thailand, Zanzibar or the Egyptian Red Sea.   
    • See silverback gorillas in the wild when you go gorilla trekking in Rwanda.  
    • Enjoy dune bashing in the Dubai desert, or climb the Sossusvlei dunes in Namibia.   
    • Visit Machu Picchu in Peru.     
    • Go deep-sea fishing in Mauritius. 
    • View wildlife in the Amazon on a safari or go game-viewing in the Kruger National Park.
    • Hop on one of our Segway Gliding Tours in Durban
    • Enjoy a Graskop Gorge Lift Experience in Mpumalanga
    • Tsitsikamma Canopy Tours is a must when visiting the Garden Route
    • Choose from a variety of Helivate Helicopter Experiences

    Ready for an adventure travel holiday? Browse our exciting adventure destinations now and let’s start planning your adventure holiday package. We've got something to suit every budget from luxury to more affordable cheap packages.
