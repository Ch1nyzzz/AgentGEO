Some common questions we get about modern intranet

A modern intranet is a cloud-based, digital workplace solution designed to enhance internal communication, knowledge sharing, and collaboration within an organization. Unlike traditional intranets, today’s best modern intranet software integrates with business apps, offers mobile access, and provides AI-powered search and automation.
Modern intranet platforms provide a centralized hub where employees can access company news, collaborate on projects, share documents, and communicate in real-time. Many top intranet solutions also integrate with tools like Microsoft Teams, Google Workspace, and Slack, making them a seamless part of daily workflows.
Companies that implement a modern workplace intranet experience: ✔ Improved employee engagement through interactive features like polls, discussions, and social feeds. ✔ Enhanced collaboration with document sharing, team spaces, and project management tools. ✔ Better knowledge management by providing a single source of truth for company policies and documents. ✔ Faster communication via instant messaging, push notifications, and AI-powered search. ✔ Seamless remote work support with mobile-friendly and cloud-based accessibility.
Yes, the best modern intranet solutions offer enterprise-grade security, including end-to-end encryption, multi-factor authentication (MFA), role-based permissions, and GDPR compliance. Many platforms are hosted on secure cloud infrastructures like AWS or Azure to ensure data protection and high availability.
